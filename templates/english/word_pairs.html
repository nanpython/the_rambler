Word Pairs

Compliment | Complement

Compliment: praise, admiration
She complimented him on his language skills.

Complement: something added to complete
The tie is a perfect complement to a suit.

Lose | Loose

Lose: misplace, fail to win, withdraw, suffer, shake off
She often loses her things because she does not remember where she left them.

Loose: not tight, sloppy, lax, informal
I prefer loose clothing.
