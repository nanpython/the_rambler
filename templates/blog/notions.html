Notions

Posted by The Rambler

Don't look for happiness, but look at the disturbances in the mind.

Don't look for enjoyment, but look at the pain in the mind.

Don't seek silence in the mind, but look at the noise in the mind.

Don't look for wisdom, but look at the immaturity in the mind.

Don't look for peace, but look at the violence in the mind.

Don't seek knowledge, but look at the ignorance in the mind.

Don't seek truth, but discover the false in the mind.

Don't try to be good, but look at the bad in the mind.

Don't seek freedom, but look at the enslavements of the mind.

Don't look at the thinker, but look at the thoughts flowing through the mind.

Do look at the human mind as a whole, of which yours is a part.
