Opposites

Posted by The Rambler

The world is a play of opposites - leaders and followers, celebrities and fans, supporters and dissenters, heroes and villains, predators and preys, winners and losers, rulers and the ruled, makers and breakers... Opposites complement each other - one exists because of the other, like the two sides of a coin.
