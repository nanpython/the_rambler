Lamp of Wisdom

Light the lamp of wisdom
Pour the oil of reason
Rein in the winds of senses
Keep it steady like a rock

Let not the lamp burn out
Lest ash smother light
And wisdom lost in a whiff
Keep vigil night and day

Matter lives and dies
In forms multitude
Its qualities endure
To build or break alike

Let not the qualities bind
The spirit rebellious within
With the light of reason rise
Above the material world

Let the light shine
The dark corners of the night
Let not a secret hide
Nor a habit stick

Let the light burn
The dross of nature thus
To keep the spirit free
Till eternity
