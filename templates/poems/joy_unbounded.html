To Joy Unbounded

Empty the mind
Of all the dust
In the night
Before you sleep

Wake up in the morn
To the dawn
Of a new day
Like a new born leaf

Die, die to yesterday
To be born again
The next day
With a clean slate

Rise, rise like pheonix
From the ashes
To life and love
To joy unbounded
