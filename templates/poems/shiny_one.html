O Shiny One

The sun in your eyes
Lifts the burden of my heart

O shiny one who are you
Why do I feel I know you

The lilt in your voice
Strums the strings of my heart

O melody queen of sound
Have I not heard you before

The grace in your gait
Quickens the beat in my heart

Like the lissom spirit of a danseuse
Comes alive in man and beast alike

Thine eyes full of light
For whom do they shine
