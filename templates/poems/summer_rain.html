Summer Rain

Mighty drums roll in the sky
Sound explodes with blinding light

The soil awaits with bated breath
And the dusty leaves of the summer earth

The cool breeze ushers twain
The bursting cloud and the gushing rain

The rushing water cleanses the earth
The glistening leaves in silent mirth

Ay, you shall feel the change in the heart
When a flower blooms in the midst of a desert
